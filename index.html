I am a mathematician, formerly working as an academic and -- as of June 2025 -- working at Timaeus on neural network alignment. I previously worked at Amherst College (2022-2025), the University of Massachusetts at Amherst (2019-2022), the IHÉS (2016-2019), and as a PhD student at Northwestern University (2010-2016). My mathematical background in academia involved the interactions between geometry, topology and mathematical physics, especially:


  • Mathematical constructions of observables in quantum field theory (factorization algebras, E_n algebras).
  • Supersymmetry and twists of supersymmetric field theory.
  • Derived geometry and the Batalin-Vlikovisky formalism.
  • The geometric Langlands correspondence and its connection to gauge theory.


A more detailed description of some of my academic work can be found in my research statement. I also have a (somewhat older) non-technical description of my research.
