Learning Seminar on String Topology

This Fall quarter at Northwestern I'm co-organising (with Brian Williams) a learning seminar in string topology. Our goal is to learn about the string topology operations on the homology of a free loop space LM, and how they arise from a partially extended 2d TFT which assigns to a point either of the Calabi-Yau algebras Omega^*(M) or C_*(Omega M).

We'll meet twice a week, on Tuesdays and Thursdays at 10am in Lunt 102.
